Embark on a unique journey in two enchanting Hong Kong’s neighbourhoods!

#ddHK will bring together a lineup of seven creative units from overseas, the Mainland and Hong Kong to enliven public space in Tsuen Wan and Sha Tau Kok, along with a series of activities filled with local flavours, inviting you to experience a unique journey in Hong Kong this winter.
