“Discovering Robin's Nest Country Park” Hiking Challenge

Dates
02.11.2024 - 28.02.2025
Venue
Robin’s Nest Country Park
The Interconnected Ecology & Landscape
Established in 2024, Robin’s Nest Country Park (RNCP) is the 25th country park in Hong Kong, covering an area of about 530 hectares. Located at Sha Tau Kok in the north-eastern New Territories, the RNCP is geographically connected with Shenzhen Wutong Mountain Scenic Area to its north and adjacent to Pat Sin Leng Country Park to its south, giving its breathtaking views. With well-preserved natural habitats, the RNCP has rich biodiversity. It also has mining and war relics, waiting for you to explore!

To introduce the newly designated Robin's Nest Country Park (RNCP) to the public, the AFCD has launched a hiking challenge at the RNCP. Participants can explore and learn about the different facets of the RNCP and challenge themselves.

Agriculture, Fisheries and Conservation Department
