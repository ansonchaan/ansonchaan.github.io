In-Depth Exploration of Double Haven (Kat O & Ap Chau)

Dates
29.12.2024 (Sun)
01.01.2025 (Wed)
12.01.2025 (Sun)
26.01.2025 (Sun) (Bilingual)
02.02.2025 (Sun)
Time
10:10 - 16:40
Places to Visit
Sha Tau Kok – Kat O – Ap Chau
Registration & Assembly Point
Registration for the event will take place on-site (10:10-10:30am at Sha Tau Kok Pier), with a limit of 20 participants per tour. Kaito ferry fare to be borne by the participants.
Professional Geopark Guides
Follow geopark guides to explore the charming villages of Double Haven
Starting from Sha Tau Kok Pier, follow the professional geopark guides as you delve into the charming village of Double Haven, where you can savor a unique landscape that integrates geology, ecology, and local culture. The villages here, including Kat O & Ap Chau, feature well-preserved natural environments, traditional historic buildings, and a rich cultural heritage. During the tour, you will have the opportunity to observe local rocks, diverse plants, and animals, gaining insight into the close relationship between geology, ecology, and culture. You will also experience the harmonious way of life of the residents coexisting with nature.

Itinerary:
10:10 - 10:30 On-site Registration at Sha Tau Kok Pier
10:30 - 11:15 Taking kaito ferry from Sha Tau Kok Pier to Kat O Pier
11:15 - 13:15 Kat O Guided Tour
13:15 - 13:30 Taking Kai kaito ferry from Kat O Pier to Ap Chau Pier
13:30 - 14:15 Lunch time (self-arranged)
14:15 - 15:15 Ap Chau Guided Tour
15:45 - 16:40 Taking kaito ferry from Ap Chau Pier to Sha Tau Kok Pier via Kat O & Lai Chi Wo
