Joyful Reading • Cultural Discovery@ Sha Tau Kok

Dates
12.12.2024 - 09.02.2025
Time
Please refer to the below image for the opening dates and hours

4 sessions of Storytelling by Story Ambassador
18.01.2025 (Sat) : 12:00 - 12:45 & 15:00 - 15:45
19.01.2025 (Sun): 12:00 - 12:45 & 15:00 - 15:45
Venue
Sha Tau Kok Promenade Sitting-out Area
An innovative outdoor reading activities for visitors’ enjoyment at a seaside space
During the #ddHK 2024-25 event period, #ddHK and Hong Kong Public Libraries jointly organise innovative outdoor reading activities “Joyful Reading • Cultural Discovery@ Sha Tau Kok” at the Sha Tau Kok Promenade Sitting-out Area. It offers over 70 titles on eco-tourism, Sha Tau Kok history, art for enjoyment by visitors in a relaxing environment, aiming to enrich the tourism experience via reading activities.

Organiser
Hong Kong Public Libraries - Sha Tau Kok Public Library
