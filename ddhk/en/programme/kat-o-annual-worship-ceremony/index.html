Kat O Annual Worship Ceremony

Dates
17.01.2025
Time
Start at 11:00
Venue
Kat O Tin Hau Temple
Experience the traditional worship ceremony in the rural village
An annual worship ceremony is held at the end of the year, during which the village takes the opportunity to thank the deities for their blessings and protection throughout the year. This ritual often involves offerings, prayers, and various traditional practices to express gratitude and seek continued favor for the upcoming year.

Kat O Village
