A semi-staged opera
Albert Herring
by Benjamin Britten

Dates
31.12.2024
Time
19:30
Venue
Tsuen Wan Town Hall, Auditorium
Follow the journey of the bashful Albert, who finds himself unexpectedly crowned May King in a delightful twist of fate
Dive into the whimsical world of Britten’s Albert Herring, a delightful comic opera that brilliantly captures the essence of small-town life with humour and heart. Follow the journey of the bashful Albert, who finds himself unexpectedly crowned May King in a delightful twist of fate. As the villagers gear up for the festivities, their quirky personalities and the weight of tradition weave a vibrant tapestry of social insight and laughter. This production brings a fresh twist by relocating the setting from a quaint English village to the lively shores of Cheung Chau, infusing the story with local flavour and charm.

We are thrilled to welcome back Terence Chang, whose direction brought such success to last year’s “Elijah.” Joined by the dynamic young conductor Jason Liu, this talented team promises to deliver a captivating performance filled with colourful characters and unforgettable music. Join us for an unforgettable experience that will leave you uplifted and inspired. Get ready to laugh, to reflect, and to embrace the magic of Albert Herring!
