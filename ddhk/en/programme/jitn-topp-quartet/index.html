JITN - TOPP Quartet

Dates
27.12.2024
Time
20:00
Venue
Tsuen Wan Town Hall Auditorium
Riyoko Takagi is known by Japanese fans as the "sexiest jazz pianist in history". She frequently transforms into anime character roles and brings a fresh twist to popular anime theme songs! Recently, she formed the TOPP Quartet with saxophonist Patrick Bartley Jr., bassist Ji Peng, and drummer Masahiko Osaka.

Patrick Bartley Jr. is a up-and-coming American jazz musician who has received Grammy nominations. He is widely recognized for his exceptional performances on the YouTube show "Live from Emmet’s Place," which has garnered millions of views.
Organiser
Jazz World (Hong Kong) Limited & Count-in Music Limited
