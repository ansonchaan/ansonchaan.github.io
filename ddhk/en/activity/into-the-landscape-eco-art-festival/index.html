Into the landscape: Eco-Art Festival

Dates
28.12.2024 (Sat)
29.12.2024 (Sun)
Time
11:00 - 20:00
Venue
Hoi Shing Garden, Tsuen Wan
Into the Landscape Eco-Art Festival is an eclectic fusion of environmental consciousness, market excitement, musical energy and family-oriented fun. There will be nearly 30 stalls covering fashion, lifestyle goods, delicacies, handicraft experiences, and more. Additionally, there will be local artists and community groups presenting busking music and art performances. Against the backdrop of the mural "Tsuen Wan: Into the Landscape" debuted in 2023 at Hoi Shing Garden, the market will be filled with vibrant energy, allowing participants to have a fulfilling and enjoyable weekend!
