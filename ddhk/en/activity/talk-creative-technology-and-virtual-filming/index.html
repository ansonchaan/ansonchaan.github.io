Tsuen Wan Creative Virtual Cinematography Experience

Dates
11.01.2025 (Sun)
Time
11:00-12:30 | 15:00-16:30
Venue
STAGEPLUS (TML Tower, Tsuen Wan)
Professional Instructor: STAGEPLUS
Virtual Production is a global trend in the creative industry that utilises high-precision LED screens to display dynamic backgrounds combined with live performances, creating an instant and realistic shooting environment. Participants will step into STAGEPLUS, Hong Kong's first virtual technology production and filming studio in Tsuen Wan. STAGEPLUS will introduce 360-degree 3D stage control technology and LED screen filming for movies and media industry, allowing participants to explore diverse scenes, three-dimensional models, and shooting angle setups, experiencing the possibilities of technology and innovative filming.
Note
  • - The workshop is conducted in Cantonese supplemented with English and Putonghua.
  • - Registration will begin at 10am on 12 December 2024 (Thu).
About STAGEPLUS
Founded in 2024, STAGEPLUS is the latest innovative extension of VPLUS STUDIO, dedicated to providing a new production framework for the media and entertainment market. As Hong Kong's first virtual technology production and filming studio, VPLUS STUDIO has introduced 360-degree StageCraft Technology and LED screen filming since early 2021, ushering in a new era of immersive storytelling.

The establishment of STAGEPLUS marks our commitment to further integrating film technology with art tech, offering centralized production facilities that open up new possibilities for a wide array of production teams. Through deep applications of technology and a passion for creative content, we not only extend art technology into physical space design and exhibitions but also enhance production quality and efficiency.
