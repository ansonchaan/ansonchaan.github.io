Planning Your Visit

Heading to Sha Tau Kok

Before departure, please apply for the Tourism Closed Area Permit.
It takes 3 working days only.
Easy steps to obtain the permit
1
Registration
HKPF Digital Services Logon[Register]
2
Application
HKPF’s online application platform 
↳Select “Closed Area Permit (CAP)”
↳Select ”Tourism Closed Area Permit (T-CAP)- individual”
↳Fill in online form and upload the application documents (Can apply for 12 persons each time)
3
Download
Login and download the electronic permit upon approval
