Collecting Objects, Memories or...?

Do you enjoy collecting? What drives you to collect— the objects themselves, or the memories they hold? Does collecting help preserve memories? The items you collect are in fact a reflection of your inner self.

“Collecting Objects, Memories or...?” will be hosted by Kit Chan, writer and photographer, along with two speakers with years of collecting experience - Hong Kong graphic designer Sandy Choi and the founder of Douguya Hatcharea, Pete Man. These experienced collectors will share their unique collections, the stories behind them, and their personal insights. This seminar may inspire you to rethink your relationship with objects and help you gain a deeper understanding of yourself.
